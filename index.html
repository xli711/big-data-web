Twitter Activity and Affordable Housing


Subject of Study

This project examines the social activity levels of residents in LIHTC developments, in comparison with those in other residential neighborhoods in the City of Philadelphia. It also looks at the sentiment expreseed by residents in LIHTC developments on social media compared to city average.


Data and Method

20,320 geotagged Tweets scraped across Philadelphia over two weeks were used for analysis. The Tweets were categorized based on the land use type of their location, and the density of Tweets were calculated using number of Tweets divided by built floor area or population.
